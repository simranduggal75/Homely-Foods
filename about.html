The Master of Cooking

We serve delicious home-cooked food on a subscription basis by leveraging a vast home chef network. The food we serve is prepared in home kitchens by passionate home chefs around us who share a love for food and a passion for cooking. Every plate of food served by Homely Foods is prepared using the freshest, top-quality ingredients based on authentic, regional recipes. Our food is also free from any kind of chemical-based colourants, taste enhancers or preservatives. We have different offerings to suit different needs for our customers - Meal Plans, Stories, Celebrations & Festivals. Meal subscriptions from Homely Foods can be customised to suit your dietary preferences and convenience. Stories by Homely Foods is a humble attempt to bring to you the story of the exciting culinary journeys of the homemakers of India. Homely Foods Celebrations is to make your special occasions extra special with our delectable party-order food options. Homely Foods Festivals is our festival offerings for all different festivals in India.
