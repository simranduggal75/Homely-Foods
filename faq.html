General FAQ

What is Homely Foods?

Homely foods offers homemade food delivery. It is a platform that makes homemade delicacies more accessible to people seeking healthy home-cooked meals prepared by home chefs.

What is Veg Thali?

The Veg Thali is a healthy, homemade meal box for your hectic schedule. Veg Thali comes with rice + dal +2 Roti +vegetable dish+ salad.

What is Non-Veg Thali?

The Veg Thali is a healthy, homemade meal box for your hectic schedule. Non-Veg Thali comes with rice + non-veg gravy +2 Roti +Dry masala chicken+ salad.

What are Meal plans ?

we have 3 different types of meal plans which are breakfast , lunch , dinner.You can go to Meal plans tab on Home page and checkout

What are the standard delivery timings? (Delivery)

The delivery for breakfast is between 9am to 11.30pm , for lunch, it is between 12 pm to 4 pm and for dinner, it is between 7 pm to 11 pm.

What are the areas covered by Homely Foods? (Delivery)

Currently, we are serviceable upto 4kms from our location(Ram krishna ashram circle)

How to give feedback to Homely Foods about the service? (feedback)

You can go to Feedback tab on the Home page , and provide feedback by filling the feedback form accordingly .

How to contact Homely Foods? (contact)
